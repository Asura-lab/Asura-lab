## Who is ASURA?

Hey — I'm **Munkhsuld**, but in orbit I go by **ASURA**.

I live where engineering meets the infinite. By day I build full-stack apps in **TypeScript & React**; by night I teach machines to **trade** and sculpt interactive worlds in **3D**. I'm happiest exploring the space between cold logic and pure imagination.

- **Currently piloting** → [Predictrix](https://github.com/Asura-lab/Forex-Signal-App), an AI that reads the Forex tape
- **Charting** → reinforcement learning for trading & portfolio optimization
- **Orbiting** → the overlap of AI, the web, and good design
- **Currently learning** → game development — leveling up in interactive worlds
- **Fun fact** → I love getting lost in thought over a hot cup of coffee
